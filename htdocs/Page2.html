After Marcos

After the rule of Marcos, the country fell into finncial ruin. The new republic was hampered by previous debt, government corruption, coup attempts, and more. For a time, it seemed that the government finances were stable, but then the 1997 Asian financial crisis hit. After that, other presidents brought economic growth for a few years.
