Before Marcos

A newly independent Third Phillipine Republic after the 2nd World War was in a state of ruin. Financial aid from the United States and some war reparations greatly helped recovery of this nation. The time in this new republic was a time of prosperity where many of our presidents enjoyed an economic boom that they lead.
